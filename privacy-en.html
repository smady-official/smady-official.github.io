Tree Under Love (the “Operator”) establishes this Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) as set forth below regarding the handling of user information obtained in the smartphone application “SMADY” (the “Service”).

1. Scope

This Policy applies to user information that the Operator obtains and uses in connection with providing the Service.

2. Information We Collect

The Operator may collect the following information in the Service. At this time, the Operator does not require users to provide information such as name, address, phone number, or email address (i.e., account registration information), except for inquiries.

2-1. Device and Usage Information

  • Device information (model, OS, language settings, time zone, app version, etc.)
  • Usage status such as in-app operation logs, screen transitions, and frequency of use
  • Log information for incident analysis (crash information, error information, etc.)
  • Event information for analytics (behavior analysis) (viewing/operation events, usage trends, etc.)
  • Network information such as connection status

2-2. Identifiers

  • Advertising identifiers (AAID/IDFA, etc.) *May be used mainly for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.

2-3. Billing (Subscription) Information

Paid features (subscriptions) in the Service are provided through App Store billing such as Apple App Store / Google Play. The Operator does not directly obtain payment information such as credit card numbers. However, in order to provide paid features and determine status (valid/expired, etc.), the Operator may handle information related to purchase status (transaction identifiers, receipt/purchase-status information, etc.) through external services (described below).

2-4. Information Provided When You Contact Us

If you contact us, we may collect your name (optional), email address, inquiry details, etc. to the extent necessary to respond.

3. How We Collect Information

  • Automatically transmitted/collected when you use the Service (logs, device information, identifiers, etc.)
  • Provided by you via input/submission (inquiries, etc.)
  • Provided by external service providers (ad delivery, purchase-status verification, etc.)

4. Purposes of Use

  1. To provide and operate the Service
  2. To improve quality and features, analyze incidents, and prevent fraud
  3. To respond to inquiries (including identity verification where necessary)
  4. To display and deliver ads, measure effectiveness, control frequency, and prevent fraud (mainly for free users)
  5. To provide subscriptions, confirm purchase status, and provide/control paid features
  6. To respond to violations of the Terms of Use, etc.
  7. For purposes incidental to the above

5. Use of External Services (SDKs, etc.) / Sharing with Third Parties

The Operator may use the following external services (including SDKs) to provide and operate the Service. In connection with such use, information may be transmitted to external service providers to the extent necessary. How external service providers handle information is governed by their respective privacy policies, etc.

  • Ad delivery: Google AdMob, etc. (depending on specifications, advertising identifiers, device information, ad impressions/click events, IP address, etc. may be transmitted.)
  • Logging / incident analysis / usage analytics: Google logging & measurement infrastructure (DataTransport, etc.) and analytics/incident-analysis tools (Firebase Crashlytics / Firebase Analytics, etc.) (crash/error information, event information, device information, usage status, etc. may be transmitted.)
  • Subscription management (verification of App Store billing status): RevenueCat, etc. (purchase status, transaction identifiers, device information, etc. may be transmitted. The Operator does not obtain payment information itself.)

6. Advertising (Free Users)

The Service may display advertisements to free users. Advertising may be stopped or reduced for users who subscribe (or otherwise qualify) according to plans designated by the Operator.

You can restrict use of advertising identifiers, disable personalized ads (or limit tracking), etc. via your Device/OS settings. Settings methods follow your Device/OS.

7. On-Device Processing (Videos, etc.)

In principle, analyses of videos, etc. in the Service are performed within your Device, and the Operator does not upload and store your video files on servers. However, this may change if you share to external services by operation, or if handling changes due to future feature additions.

8. Permissions

The Service may use the following permissions to provide features.

  • Access to the Device camera (shooting/analysis, etc.)
  • Access to photos/videos on the Device (selecting/analyzing videos, etc.)

*If you do not grant permissions, some or all features may be unavailable.

9. Security Measures

The Operator will take necessary and appropriate measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of collected information and to ensure secure management. (Details of such measures will be provided individually within a reasonable scope in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.)

10. Retention Period

The Operator retains collected information for the period necessary to achieve the purposes of use, and after achieving such purposes will delete or anonymize it within a reasonable scope. Retention periods on external services follow the rules of each provider.

11. Disclosure / Correction / Suspension of Use / Deletion

In accordance with applicable laws, you may request disclosure, correction, suspension of use, deletion, etc. of information about yourself that the Operator holds. However, because the Service does not require account registration, the information held by the Operator may be limited, and the scope of possible responses may also be limited. If you have a request, please contact us via “13. Contact for Inquiries.”

12. Use by Minors

Minors must obtain consent from a parent/guardian or other legal representative before using the Service.
